IESL Student Chapter of University of Moratuwa

The IESL Student Chapter of the University of Moratuwa is a pre-dominant Student Chapter established under IESL-Young Members Section. Bridging the void between Engineering Professionals and the dynamic undergraduate community in the university, we hope to confer proficient engineering exposure to all engineering undergraduates at the University of Moratuwa. Through our annual initiatives, we aspire to encourage community service, local and international recognition, and professional development while taking the undergraduate community to greater heights by identifying their skills and providing them with exceptional opportunities to excel as professionals as well as individuals.

About Us

Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka

Established in 1956, IESL the apex body of engineers in Sri Lanka was found with the objective of providing indispensable advancements of science and practice of engineering, across the country.

Vision

Be amongst the leading professional institutions in engineering and technology in the world.

Mission

To be the apex national body of engineers in Sri Lanka which ensures internationally recognized and locally relevant standards in the professional practice and education in engineering, while actively supporting national development and diligently serving its members and the society at large.

Young Members Section

The YMS Committee is recognized as the invigoration that unites the young engineers and IESL together. Its programs and work priorities are devised as an interface for all the young members to excel in their careers of becoming eminent Engineers under the guidance of IESL.
