About Us

Welcome to the IESL Students chapter of University of Moratuwa. IESL Students chapter of University of Moratuwa is a well-reputed students chapter, established along with IESL-YMS. We try to confer professional engineering exposure to all engineering undergraduates at the University of Moratuwa.

Since 2011, the IESL students chapter of University of Moratuwa has been helping undergraduates developing their skills and providing them with exceptional opportunities to excel through competitions, talks and discussions, webinars, and field visits while encouraging them to engage in community service projects as well.

We help undergraduates identify their potential career paths through events like 'paving path' while offering them the perfect opportunities to develop their engineering-related skills by organizing competitions like 'Techno-Hackothon' and 'Evotech'. Moreover, we offer many webinars and field visits to university undergraduates, opening up many possibilities to enhance their knowledge. There's nothing more rewarding than giving back and making a difference in the community, thus projects carried out by the IESL Student Chapter of University of Moratuwa are exceptionally devised with the intention to motivate the younger generation and to help them choose the right career path for a brighter future.
